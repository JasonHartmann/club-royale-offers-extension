Our Products

Flagship

CompRoyale

The smartest way to analyze your Royal Caribbean comp cruise offers.

CompRoyale is a browser extension that transforms the Club Royale Offers page into a powerful, sortable, filterable dashboard. Compare offers across ships, dates, and categories — spot the best deals instantly with advanced search, date-range filtering, back-to-back sailing detection, and one-click CSV export.

  • Sortable & groupable offer tables
  • Advanced multi-field search with date-range picker
  • Back-to-Back sailing builder & chain detection
  • Favorites, linked profiles & combined offer views
  • CSV export for offline analysis
  • Cross-browser: Chrome, Edge, Firefox & Safari
